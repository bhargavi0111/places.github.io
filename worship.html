ISKCON Temple

Situated in the heart of the Rajajinagar area, the ISKCON Temple stands as a splendid homage to Lord Krishna. Its architectural grandeur seamlessly fuses modernity with tradition, resulting in a captivating marvel. Within the temple's expansive premises, the main deity, Lord Krishna, is reverently enshrined alongside Radha in an opulent sanctum sanctorum. Beyond the spiritual core, the temple complex offers a multifaceted experience, featuring a Vedic Cultural Centre that imparts ancient wisdom, a charming gift shop, and a vegetarian restaurant renowned for serving delectable meals.

Bull Temple

Located in the serene Basavanagudi area, the Bull Temple stands as one of Bangalore's ancient landmarks. The temple's centerpiece is a monumental Nandi, the sacred mount of Lord Shiva, skillfully carved from a single granite rock. This awe-inspiring statue reaches an impressive 15 feet in height and spans 20 feet in length. Devotees from far and wide gather at this sacred site to fervently offer prayers to Lord Shiva, seeking blessings for strength and wisdom in their spiritual journey.

Banashankari Temple

Located in the Banashankari area, this temple is devoted to Goddess Banashankari, a divine incarnation of Goddess Parvati. The temple stands out for its distinctive architectural style, seamlessly blending elements of the Dravidian and Vijayanagara styles. Devotees frequent this sacred abode to seek the benevolent blessings of the goddess, praying for well-being and prosperity in their lives.
